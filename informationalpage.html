Welcome to my informational page about the 2005-2013 C6 Corvette


The C6 Chevrolet Corvette was produced from 2005-2013 as the 6th generation of Chevrolet's famous Corvette line. It included trim models from highest end to lowest end:

  1. ZR1
    • Comes with 638 Horsepower and 605 lb/ft of torque.
  2. Z06
    • Comes with 505 Horsepower and 470 lb/ft of torque
  3. Grandsport
    • Comes with 430 Horsepower and 424 lb/ft of torque
  4. Z51
    • Comes with 400 Horsepower and 400 lb/ft of torque

This legendary generation of Corvettes came with Naturally Aspirated LS7 V8 Engines (The ZR1 came with a supercharged LS7) mounted in a lightweight chassis, making it a true American performance muscle car. If you would like to read more please go to
